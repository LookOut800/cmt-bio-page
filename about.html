Charles Talbot

Artist | Potter | Engineer

Artist Bio

Charles Talbot is a visual artist, ceramicist, and engineer. Growing up in Essex, Massachusetts, by the ocean, he was influenced by the rhythms of nature and craft, shaped by a family of gardeners and sailors. His work blends traditional ceramics, material experimentation, and digital processes.

Artist Statement

By conflicting real or mythic points of origin, traditions of American identity, and personal lineage, my work gathers narrative into an increasingly singular entropic condition. Through abstraction, I examine my place in these histories and distill them into sensations about history.

Engineering & Digital Art

Charles is also a Principal Software Engineer, specializing in UI/UX, front-end development, and creative coding. His dual expertise allows him to explore emerging areas of digital fabrication, computational design, and interactive installations.
